Collaboration Models

Model A: Tech Hub Partner

Converting Hardware Grants Into Learning Outcomes

  • Designation: NSA handles software/training for 100 district grant schools.
  • Execution: 3-day cert workshop, deployment of licenses.
  • Reporting: Quarterly automated data feeds directly into MoEST.
  • Budget: Software/Training easily fits inside the 20-30% curriculum budget of the 2.5M NPR grant.
Collaboration Models

Model B: Teacher Bank Partner

Making the Teacher Bank Genuinely Effective

  • Pre-Service Training: NSA trains Teacher Bank CS volunteers before deployment.
  • Equipped: They graduate with school access credentials and pre-structured lessons.
  • Ongoing Network: Online refresher modules provided monthly.
  • Year 1 Target: 200 Teacher bank volunteers trained.

Model C: Curriculum Endorsement

Official MoEST Resource Listing

  • Petition: NSA submits formal full CDC Grade 9-10 alignment docs.
  • Practical Outcome: Schools can purchase without friction from local budgets.
  • Integration: NSA lesson plans become the reference framework for CDC's teaching hours.
